How long does it take to make order?

The execution time of the order depends on the three factors: the complexity of the picture, the style of spelling and size. The average order execution time is 7 days excluding delivery time.In some cases At the request of the customer, you can reduce the term of up to 5 days.

Note: if you decide something change in the picture, make adjustments - this can take an extra time that does not It is taken into account with the agreed period of execution.

Can I see the picture before her receipt?

Yes.By readiness, we send by e -mail the digital photo of your picture, in order So that you can, if necessary, make your adjustments and wishes, if any.You You can also see the picture "live" in our salon.Tell your manager about this.

What format should be photos

We work with all known images!You can download JPEG through the site, TIFF, PNG in size up to 50 megabytes.You must have copyrights or have permission for Using the image.If you have a different image format (raw, pdf), then you can Use FTP bootloader.If you want to take a photo from paper photography (not Digital), send it to us, we will scan, process and return the photo along with your order!

Can I give special instructions artist
